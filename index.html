P.J Joseph

P. J. Joseph is an Indian politician, former minister and MLA, representing the Thodupuzha assembly constituency. He entered Kerala politics through Kerala Congress in 1968. In 1970, Joseph contested and won his first election from Thodupuzha to the legislative assembly. He won these elections on nine occasions (1970, 1977, 1980, 1982, 1987, 1996, 2006, 2011, 2016).
